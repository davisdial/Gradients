All About Gradients

Gradients are a wonderful design tool that have been in use for decades, but have recently been revitalized by new trends in web design

Gradients were basically becoming a forgotten tool in the web development world throughout the 2000's and into the 2010's. But in 2016, Instagram introduced their new app icon, whcih featured a bright and dominating gradient. Since then, gradients are basically ubiquitous in web-dev. So now, gradients are IN once again! Wahoo!






Take a look at some trippy radial gradients! Radial gradients consist of a blend of colors radiating from an epicenter.

See the Pen Trippy Radial Gradients by Ben Briggs (@ben-eb) on CodePen.




You can also implement entire background gradients to give a light, professional tint to your webpage. These linear gradients, unlike radial ones, blend colors across a straight axis.

See the Pen Fun with background gradients by aepicos (@aepicos) on CodePen.




Modern hover effects give sites extra layers of quality and harmony, especially when you use a continuous color pallet across your page.

See the Pen CSS Gradient Hover Effect by Jon Daiello (@jondaiello) on CodePen.
